# Nunus Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 9, 2026

Nunus — *Read only the news that's new* — is a browser extension for Safari (iOS and Mac), Chrome, and Firefox. It grays out news headlines you have already seen. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data stored locally

- **Viewed article titles** — stored in your browser's extension storage so Nunus can gray out headlines on future visits.
- **User preferences** — such as topic-blocking phrases.

All data remains on your device in your browser's local extension storage. Nothing is sent to any server.

## No tracking

Nunus has no analytics, no telemetry, no accounts, and no cookies. The extension makes no network requests of its own.

## Third-party services

Nunus does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL.
